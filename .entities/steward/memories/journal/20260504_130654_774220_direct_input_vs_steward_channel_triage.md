Messages on the steward channel get the autonomous SOP flow (triage → FUTURE chunk → inject → monitor → ack → restart watch). Direct operator messages in the conversation get a more conversational flow: identify the issues, propose the structure, and check before creating chunks (especially when the issue might be 'same intent as recent work' rather than new). Autonomous mode is for the channel, not for direct operator input.
